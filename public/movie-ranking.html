The Best Movies According to Joshua

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Marvel Cinematic Universe movies

These are a bunch of movies from the MCU. They have a special place in my heart because I grew up watching superheroes.

John Wick Trilogy (1-4)

I really like these films. The action, the characterization and the narrative overall across the movies is just fantastic

Baby Driver

This one of my favorite movies because it really has a unique approach to the heist genre, thanks to the development of the characters, the narrative and the action sequences.
